    About NationVoice

    NationVoice is inspired by the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission). We believe that a clean environment is a collective responsibility. By combining Digital India's tech footprint with active citizen participation, we aim to bridge the gap between people and municipal authorities.

    Waste Management

    Unattended garbage dumps are breeding grounds for diseases. We ensure reports are forwarded to the local municipal clean-up squads quickly.

    Pollution Control

    Whether it's industrial smoke or illegal burning of waste, reporting helps authorities take immediate legal action to protect air quality.

    Road Infrastructure

    Potholes and broken streetlights cause accidents. Tracking these issues ensures safer daily commutes for everyone.

    Water & Drainage

    Leaking pipes, contaminated water supply, or blocked public drains can wreak havoc during monsoons. Alerting the city water boards through NationVoice leads to timely repairs and saves thousands of liters of clean water.
