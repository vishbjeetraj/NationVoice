How NationVoice Works

A simple three-step process to ensure transparency and rapid resolution.

📸

1. Snap & Report

See a problem? Take a photo, write a brief description, and choose the correct category (e.g. Garbage, Roads).

🔍

2. Get Tracking ID

You automatically receive a unique Complaint ID to monitor real-time progress on our portal.

✅

3. Action Taken

Local authorities review the issue and update the status from 'Pending' to 'Resolved' upon completion.
